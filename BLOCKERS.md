## Escalation Process
If a blocker remains unresolved for more than 1 business day:
1. Escalate to project governance owner.
2. Update `STATUS.md` risks and mitigation actions.
3. Add escalation context to `BITACORA.md`.
4. Re-prioritize impacted tasks in `PLAN.md`.
5. Close blocker only after documenting resolution evidence.
